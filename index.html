Historcle: A History Guessing Game

I have thought of a secret historical figure. Can you guess him? For each guess I will tell you how many years apart the two figures were born, and how far away they were born. Please note that as I am pulling these figures from Wikipedia,you must be precise in your spelling, i.e. "LeBron James" matches but not "Lebron James". The "hints" are commonalities between the two people. For example, if both your guess and my secret person were writers, then "writer" will appear as a hint.

This game is just like wordle, in that there will be a new secret historical figure to guess each day. Right now we are on day . The next day starts in hours.

By the way, this game is quite challenging because there are very many historical figures (at least 1000), so if you need to use Wikipedia to cheat that's ok.
